##### Audio player with many cool ways to rewind and seek 

Quick use and simple design.
Great for reading before sleep or during jogging.
Great for playing very long tapes... I mean audio files.
3h lecture, 20h long audiobook - no problem, you can still navigate easily.
As minimalistic as you like.

Most audio-players use very small fonts with fancy shadows and gradients, so I need to wear my glasses (e.g.: to read current time).
Come on, not having to use glasses while reading in bed is the main advantage of audiobooks
